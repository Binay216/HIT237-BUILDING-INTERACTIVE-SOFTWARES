#  Section 1: Terms and Conditions of Group Work

## 1. Group Allocation (Marks Distribution)
All members are expected to contribute fairly and consistently. Marks will be distributed equally only if all members meet agreed contribution standards. If a member fails to contribute significantly, the group reserves the right to report this to the teaching team, supported by GitHub commit history and communication records.



## 2. Communication
The team will use Microsoft Teams for meetings and communication, and GitHub for task tracking and version control. All members are expected to respond within 5 hours. Weekly meetings will be conducted to review progress, discuss issues, and plan next steps.

---

## 3. Task Ownership
Tasks will be divided based on individual strengths and assigned through mutual agreement. Each member is responsible for completing their assigned tasks on time. If a member is unable to meet deadlines, they must inform the group in advance. Repeated failure to contribute will be escalated to the lecturer.
